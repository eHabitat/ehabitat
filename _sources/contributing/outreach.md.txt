# Outreach

:::{caution} Page in construction
:::

You can also contribute to eHabitat by doing outreach, writing blogposts, getting
people to use it at your company or university, helping external libraries integrate with eHabitat (in
any programming language of your choice).

If you have a website or blog where you often post about eHabitat, a library that integrates with eHabitat reach out to us or
open a PR to add your blog to {ref}`community`, we'd love to hear from you!
